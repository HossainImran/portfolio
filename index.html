About

Hey there, I'm a Front-end Web Developer and an UX/UI Designer with experience in working on few mobile games and couple of local clients websites and applications. I design and code beautifully simple things, and I love what I do.

I value simple content structure, clean design patterns, and thoughtful interactions. I like to code things from scratch, and enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser. I genuinely care about people, and love helping fellow designers work on their craft.

I'm quietly confident, naturally curious, and perpetually working on improving my chops one design problem at a time. I have always been fascinated by the intersection of design and technology, and I am thrilled to be able to bring my creative ideas to life through coding and designing.
